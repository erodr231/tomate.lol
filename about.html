About

What is a Pomodoro timer?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method where you work in focused bursts, usually 25 minutes followed by a short 5 minute break. Rinse and repeat! It helps you stay productive without burning out.

Where did the Pomodoro Technique come from?

It was created by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s while he was a university student. He used a tomato-shaped kitchen timer (pomodoro is Italian for tomato) to break his study sessions into focused intervals. What started as a personal productivity hack eventually became one of the most popular time management techniques in the world!

Why should I use tomate.lol?
